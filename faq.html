General Information

Q: What types of dance classes do you offer?

A: We offer a variety of classes, including Ballet, Jazz, Tap, Hip Hop, Contemporary, Ballroom, Belly Dance, Irish Dance and recreational programs. Check our class schedule for specific offerings.

Q: What age is required?

A: We have classes for all ages, from toddlers to adults. Our classes are grouped by age and skill level to ensure everyone gets the most out of their experience.

Q: Do I need previous experience to join?

A: We offer beginner classes for those with no experience, intermediate and advanced levels for seasoned dancers.

Q: What do I need to bring to class?

A: Bring water, a towel, and required dance shoes. We recommend a small bag for your belongings.

Q: How long are the classes?

A: Most classes are 45 minutes to 1 hour long.

Q: What is your cancellation policy?

A: Cancellations must be made at least 24 hours before the class to receive a dance credit. Missed classes are non-refundable.
